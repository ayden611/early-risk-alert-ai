# Pilot Evidence Packet — DUA-Safe Public Version

## Purpose

This public packet summarizes aggregate validation evidence for Early Risk Alert AI.

## DUA-Safe Boundary

Public evidence may include:

- Aggregate validation metrics
- Threshold comparison tables
- Alert reduction summaries
- False-positive-rate summaries
- Detection summaries
- Median lead-time summaries
- Methodology notes
- Governance and claim guardrails
- Sanitized examples without real MIMIC identifiers or exact case-linked timestamps

Public evidence must not include:

- Raw MIMIC CSV files
- Row-level enriched exports
- Real MIMIC identifiers
- Exact case-linked timestamps
- Patient-level rows
- Any row-level derived restricted data

## Current Validation Story

Retrospective analysis on de-identified MIMIC data showed ERA can reduce alert burden while maintaining configurable event-cluster detection and explainable review context across threshold settings.

## Pilot-Safe Framing

Decision support only. Retrospective analysis only. Not intended to diagnose, direct treatment, replace clinician judgment, or independently trigger escalation.

## Claims to Avoid

- ERA predicts deterioration
- ERA detects crises early
- ERA prevents adverse events
- ERA outperforms standard monitoring
- ERA replaces clinician judgment
- ERA independently triggers escalation
