How To Read This Packet

This packet summarizes retrospective validation evidence for pilot-readiness review. It should be read as workflow-support evidence, not as a prospective clinical-performance claim.

ERA is positioned as a selective review-prioritization layer that reduces unnecessary review interruptions.

2. Read lead time carefully.
