Executive Summary

Early Risk Alert AI transforms fragmented monitoring workflows into a unified command-center model. The platform combines live patient visibility, AI-assisted risk prioritization, hospital-facing dashboards, and downloadable investor materials into a single enterprise-ready system.

What we solve

  • Delayed detection of patient deterioration
  • Fragmented remote monitoring infrastructure
  • Manual clinician triage across high patient volumes
  • Limited executive visibility into risk and operational load

Why now

  • Hospitals need scalable intelligence layers, not just raw data feeds
  • Remote patient monitoring adoption continues to expand
  • Health systems are under pressure to reduce avoidable acute events
  • AI-enabled command centers create a differentiated enterprise workflow
